# Orch Blueprint

Orch is the internal orchestration and learning layer for KasiLink. It remains the core engine: multi-agent coordination, moderation, memory, auditability, APIs, and deployment surfaces.

Orch now also grows an additive layer called **Orch Labs**. Orch Labs is not a product-direction shift. It is the visible experiment studio on top of orch, modeled after a Google-Labs-style portfolio of practical AI tools for South African life.

## Operating Model

- **orch core:** orchestration, tool routing, moderation, memory, audit, APIs
- **Orch Labs:** experiment registry, public-impact tool catalog, phase roadmap
- **SA tools:** jobs, utilities, community support, education, health, language, and accessibility

## Mission Expansion

Orch must support:
- South Africa-focused AI tools that solve concrete daily problems
- all official South African languages as a critical capability
- accessibility-first interaction, including speech-impairment-aware workflows
- promotion of successful experiments from Labs into stable production products
