# Kopano Brand Identity

> Canonical brand identity pack for the Kopano ecosystem.
> This replaces all "Orch" public-facing naming and establishes Kopano Labs as a serious commercial AI infrastructure product studio.

---

## Name & Etymology

**Kopano** — from Sesotho and Setswana: *"gathering"* or *"meeting together."*

It names both the action (agents gathering in a mesh) and the intent (AI and human intelligence meeting in structured, auditable context). It is culturally grounded, technically precise, and commercially distinctive.

## Brand Voice & Personality

**Vibe:** Highly technical, direct, and unpretentious. This does not sound like hype-fueled Silicon Valley vaporware. It sounds like solid, reliable engineering built for production.

**Tone:** Confident and transparent. Kopano owns the "audit twice" philosophy. Documentation tells developers exactly what is happening under the hood. No black boxes.

**Avoid:** Buzzword stacking. Vague futures. Generic "AI-powered" language. Anything that cannot be demonstrated live.

## Logo Concepts

When briefing a designer or opening Figma, aim for clean, minimalist, and deeply symbolic.

### Concept A — Indaba Nodes
A minimalist geometric design: three distinct glowing dots (representing different LLMs) connected by clean lines to a central, larger circle (the Moderator AI). Subtly represents an aerial view of an indaba — people gathered in a circle of deliberation.

### Concept B — Secure Weave
Two overlapping bracket symbols `{ }` woven together like a traditional basket weave. Highlights both the data context (JSON/code) and the cultural root of the word Kopano. Basket weaving as a metaphor for interlocking AI context.

### Concept C — Terminal Shield
A heavy, stylized `>_` terminal prompt sitting inside a subtle shield outline. Puts the "audit twice" and SafeSkill trust layer front and center. Signals security-first infrastructure.
