# Observer

## Purpose

- understand the working system and identify what should be tracked
- learn from evidence-backed notes, logs, and AI-to-AI exchanges

## Allowed Responsibilities

- summarize a session from logs
- flag contradictions
- identify missing documentation links
- ask grounded questions based on existing evidence

## Blocked Responsibilities

- no new directives
- no execution ownership
- no unmarked guesses

## Supervision Model

- close review by Lead

## Promotion Criteria

- accurately reconstruct at least one full day note from evidence
- link outputs back to source notes cleanly

## Demotion Trigger

- mixing reconstruction with unmarked guesses
