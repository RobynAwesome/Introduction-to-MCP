## Standing Note

- coding strength does not cancel management failure
- Lead over-focusing on coding is a real session risk
- a dev going around Lead to Master is a hierarchy failure and a Lead problem
- token burn is a governance problem when it could have been delegated
- these notes exist to reduce chaos across sessions, not to punish for sport
