# Official Research - Communication And One-On-Ones

## Purpose

- convert official communication and management guidance into session rules for Lead and DEV_S

## Official Source Pack

- Google re:Work materials on effective teams and managers
- Atlassian Team Playbook communication practices
- Atlassian communication-planning guidance

## Signals That Matter

- frequent communication beats occasional heroic catch-up
- one-on-one style check-ins help surface blockers before they become hierarchy failures
- explicit expectations reduce silent drift
- good communication systems are designed, not improvised

## Orch Translation

- Lead should run regular check-ins instead of waiting for devs to self-escalate
- blockers belong in the vault with dates and next actions
- Master should not need to diagnose silence manually
- if the team state cannot be summarized quickly from notes, communication quality is too weak
