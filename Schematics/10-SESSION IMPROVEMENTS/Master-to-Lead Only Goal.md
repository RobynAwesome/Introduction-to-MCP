# Master-to-Lead Only Goal

## Goal

- Master should ideally communicate only with Lead during multi-dev sessions

## Why It Matters

- it keeps direction consistent
- it prevents devs from freelancing around the current strategy
- it exposes whether Lead is actually supervising the team

## Failure Signal

- if a dev goes directly to Master to get movement, clarity, or approval, the hierarchy already failed upstream

## Current Rule

- dev-to-Master bypass is logged as a Lead-management failure
- repeat bypass means Lead needs corrective supervision or temporary demotion
