# Dev Escalation And Discipline Ladder

## Level 1 | Clarify

- use when the dev is blocked, vague, or late but still inside trust

## Level 2 | Narrow Scope

- use when the dev starts drifting or touching adjacent lanes

## Level 3 | Evidence Required

- use when the dev reports progress that is no longer safe to trust casually

## Level 4 | Standby

- use when there is no actionable next step or the lane is externally blocked

## Level 5 | Removal

- use for destructive overwrite, fabricated progress, or repeated chain-of-command failure

## Rule

- do not punish blocked devs for not having code that does not exist
- move blocked devs to standby early and explicitly
